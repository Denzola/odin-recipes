Pikliz

Home

Description

Pikliz is a Haitian pickled slaw with cabbage, bell peppers, carrots, and Scotch bonnet pepper that brightens up every dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 2 red or yellow bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 medium red onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Stuff cabbage, carrot, bell peppers, onion, scallions, Scotch bonnet pepper, allspice, peppercorn, cloves, and bay leaf into a mason jar with a lid until it's filled to the brim.
  2. Add vinegar, and lime juice and press down on the ingredients. Seal the jar and shake once.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 days for flavors to meld.
